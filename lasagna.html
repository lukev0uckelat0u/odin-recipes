Pumpkin Lasagna

Home

A vegetarian lasagna full of autumnal comfort

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna pasta sheets
  • Pumpkin cut into 1cm slices
  • Bechamel sauce
  • Scamorza cheese, grated
  • Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Garlic
  • Fresh rosemary
  • Freshly grated nutmeg
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Sea salt
  1. Grate garlic into Bechamel sauce
  2. Ladle a spoon of bechamel sauce into base of cooking tray
  3. layer sheets of dried lasagna sheets to cover base
  4. Add another ladle of Bechamel sauce
  5. Add Scamorza cheese
  6. Cover cheese with layer of pumpkin
  7. Throw a few sprigs of rosemary on top
  8. Add small amount of salt, pepper and grated nutmeg
  9. Cover with grated Parmesan cheese
  10. Repeat steps 3 to 8
  11. Add a final layer or Bechamel, Scamorza and Parmesan
  12. Bake until golden.
